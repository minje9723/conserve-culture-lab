About Us

Welcome to the Conservation Science LAB. (CSL)! We are a passionate group dedicated to applying scientific methods to the preservation of cultural heritage. Our research focuses on materials and technologies used for the conservation and restoration of artifacts, aiming to protect our shared history for future generations.

Our Mission

  • To advance conservation science through innovative research.
  • To foster interdisciplinary collaboration and knowledge exchange.
  • To train the next generation of conservation scientists.

We believe in a hands-on approach, combining rigorous scientific inquiry with a deep respect for art and artifacts.
