Research Facilities

State-of-the-art analytical equipment and research facilities

X-ray Fluorescence Spectrometer (XRF)

Analytical Equipment Available

State-of-the-art XRF equipment for non-destructive elemental analysis of cultural heritage objects.

Key Specifications

  • Detectable Elements: Na(11) ~ U(92)
  • Measurement Time: 10 seconds ~ 10 minutes
  • Detection Limit: 0.1% or less

Main Applications

  • Metallic artifact composition analysis
  • Pigment and paint analysis
  • Ceramic body analysis

Scanning Electron Microscope (SEM-EDS)
